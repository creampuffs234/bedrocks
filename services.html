Our Services

We offer a variety of specialized services to cater to our patients' healthcare needs. Below is a list of our available services.

Cardiology

Our Cardiology department provides comprehensive heart care services, including diagnostics and treatments for various heart conditions.

Neurology

We specialize in treating neurological disorders, offering advanced care for conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.

Orthopedics

Our Orthopedics department provides expert care for bone, joint, and muscle disorders, ensuring you stay active and pain-free.

Patient Registration
